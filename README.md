# Trace

**Walk it. Reveal it. Keep it.**

Trace is a fog-of-war exploration app for iPhone built with Expo. Your whole map starts dark — every street you walk, run, or bike clears hexagon tiles permanently, turning real-world movement into a personal map of everywhere you've actually been.

1. Map Hero  
- Headline: WALK TO REVEAL YOUR CITY  
- Subtext: Every step uncovers new hex tiles.
2. Live Session (walk/run active)  
- Headline: TRACK WALKS IN REAL TIME  
- Subtext: Distance, pace, and new tiles while you move.
3. Social Proof (map with demo friend tiles visible)  
- Headline: SEE FRIEND TERRITORY  
- Subtext: Compare nearby claims and expand your zone.
4. Progress / Stats screen  
- Headline: BUILD YOUR STREAK  
- Subtext: Daily momentum, revisit intensity, total area revealed.
5. Activities / History (or Share card)  
- Headline: SAVE EVERY SESSION  
- Subtext: Review routes and share standout walks.
